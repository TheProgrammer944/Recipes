French toast

  • Time: 3 to 8 minutes
  • Servings: 2 to 4
Ingredients Quantity
eggs 2 to 3
milk 1 cup
sugar 1 teaspoon
salt 1 teaspoon

steps:

  1. crack your eggs in a washed, unused cereal boul.
  2. pour the milk in the boul.
  3. whisk the batter.
  4. add the sugar and salt.
  5. mix the batter a little more.
  6. dip the bread into the batter.
  7. Put thr french toast on the pan
  8. put the pan on the stove
  9. set the stove on high flame
  10. flip the bread after it has stayed on the pan for 3 minutes
  11. turn off the stove and put the french toast on a plate.
  12. Enjoy the food!!!

Bluberry Pancackes

  • Time: 5 to 10 minutes
  • serving: 3 to 5
Ingredients
Quantity
Flour
10 cups
milk
1 cup
blueberries
3 to 5 per pancacke
baking powder
1 to 2 teaspoons
brown sugar
1/2 a cup
powdered sugar
1 teaspoon per pancacke

steps:

  1. dump the flour into a big boul.
  2. pour the milk into the boul.
  3. dump brown sugar.
  4. empty the teaspon of baking powder into the bowl.
  5. mix the batter.
  6. power some of the batter in a plastic bag with a sharp corner.
  7. cut the corner.
  8. spread some butter on a pan.
  9. make sure the butter is melted on the pan.
  10. use the bag you used to make a circle on the buttered pan.
  11. keep the stove on high flame and leave the panckace on there for 3 minutes.
  12. flip the pancacke.
  13. leave it there for 3 minutes again.
  14. put the pancacke on a plate.
  15. (optional) add honey and/or maple syrup.
  16. add one of your favourite small fruit like blueberries.
  17. put the powdered sugar on the pancacke.
  18. Enjoy the food!!!

Green tea




Ingredients
Quantity
water
1 cup
green tea bag
1 bag

steps:

  1. get that one cup of water and pour it in a cup if not done yet.
  2. pour that water in a kettle.
  3. kettle makes no more sounds, pour the hot water carfully into a cup.
  4. get or buy a teabag that has a green plastic bag around it.
  5. put the teabag into the water.
  6. mix the water.
  7. make sure the tea is yellow or any other color but the water color should not be transparent by the time you are done.
  8. take a little sip to see if it is good.
  9. (optional) if you dont like it, then try to offer it to your siblings or parents
  10. enjoy the green tea!!!
